1975 Copa América squads

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search
List of footballers

These are the squads for the countries that played in the 1975 Copa América. The first round was played in three groups of three teams with Uruguay, receiving a bye to the semi finals.[1][2]
