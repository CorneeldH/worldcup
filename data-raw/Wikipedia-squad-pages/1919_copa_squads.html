1919 South American Championship squads

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search
Football squads

The following are the squads of national teams that played in the 1919 South American Championship. The participating countries were Argentina, Brazil, Chile and Uruguay.[1] The teams played in a single round-robin tournament, earning two points for a win, one point for a draw, and zero points for a loss.
